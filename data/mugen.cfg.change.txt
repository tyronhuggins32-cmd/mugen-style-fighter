MUGEN.CFG CHANGE
================

Do not replace the full official data/mugen.cfg with this file.

Open the existing file in your M.U.G.E.N installation:

    data/mugen.cfg

Inside the [Options] section, find the motif line and change only that line to:

    motif = data/puredef/system.def

Recommended display settings for this first motif:

    GameWidth = 1280
    GameHeight = 720

The matching coordinate setting inside system.def is:

    localcoord = 1280,720
